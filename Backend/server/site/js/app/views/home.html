What is Pomoc?

Pomoc is a B2B service that allows vendors to easily integrate live support services into their mobile applications.

Why Use Pomoc?

When users of their mobile applications face a problem, they can activate a button to fire up the Pomoc widget, where they can get support from the vendors’ customer service representatives in real-time, probably through text or voice chat.

Customer service representatives can use the Pomoc mobile application where they are presented with a dashboard and to answer customer queries in real-time.

Watch the video to find out more!

Unintrusive Support Widget

Screen real estate on mobile is limited. It is important that users are able to get their questions answered, and also remedy the problem at the same time. The Pomoc support widget is designed in a way that takes up little space on the mobile application screen and provides unintrusive support from the application creator.

Customer Contextual Information

The more information the customer service rep knows about the customer, the easier it will be for him/her to help the customer. Developers can configure the customer’s device and user information to be sent to the service rep when customers activate the Pomoc widget. This gives the service rep some context regarding the issue and will aid in the troubleshooting.

Screenshots and Annotation

Often, the troubleshooting process will be easier if the customer service reps can see the device screen of the customer. Pomoc makes it really easy for customers to share screenshots of their device. Customer service reps on the other end, upon receipt of the screenshot, can annotate the image and send the screenshot back to the user.
