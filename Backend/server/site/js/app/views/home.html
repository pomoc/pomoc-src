What is Pomoc?

Pomoc is a B2B service that allows vendors to easily integrate live support services into their mobile applications.

Why Use Pomoc?

When users of their mobile applications face a problem, they can activate a button to fire up the Pomoc widget, where they can get support from the vendors’ customer service representatives in real-time, probably through text or voice chat.

Customer service representatives can use the Pomoc mobile application where they are presented with a dashboard and to answer customer queries in real-time.
